Flower Garden - Full Instructions

Goal

  • Earn coins by planting, watering, harvesting, and collecting coins.
  • Reach the round objective before time runs out.
  • Level up to increase challenge and rewards.

Movement

  • Desktop: Move with W/A/S/D or Arrow keys.
  • Mobile/Tablet: Use the on-screen D-pad.
  • You can only move inside your own private plot area.

Planting and Growing

  • Select a seed from the Plant Seed dropdown.
  • Plant with Space/E, click/tap in the garden, or use Plant touch button.
  • Plants grow over time from seed to mature flower.
  • Click mature flowers to harvest reward coins.

Watering

  • Use R key, Water button, or Water touch button.
  • Water affects the nearest growing plant in your plot.
  • Watered plants grow faster.

Coins

  • Coins spawn in the garden and from plants.
  • Collect by moving near coins or clicking/tapping them.
  • Coins are used to buy seeds and trade.

Trading and Shop

  • Open Shop to buy seeds directly with coins.
  • Use Trade controls to buy/sell seeds with other gardeners.
  • Select partner, seed type, and quantity, then choose Buy or Sell.

Community Gardeners

  • Each gardener has a private plot and their own resources.
  • NPC gardeners automatically plant, harvest, and trade.
  • Use F key, Wave button, or Wave touch button to wave.

Rounds, Levels, and Timer

  • HUD shows current level, objective progress, and remaining time.
  • Meeting objective increases level and bonus rewards.
  • If time runs out, level drops and the round resets.

Mobile and Touch

  • Use bottom controls: D-pad + Plant/Water/Wave/Help/Home.
  • All actions are available without keyboard.

Save, Reset, and Exit

  • Progress saves automatically in browser local storage.
  • Reset clears saved game and restarts from beginning.
  • Use Home to return directly to Game Hub.
